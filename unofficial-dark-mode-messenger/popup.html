Charcoal for Messenger

How to use:
  • Click on the Messenger icon on the top left of the web page to access preferences
  • Choose between 3 themes: Charcoal, Midnight, and Deep Blue
  • Toggle dark mode on/off or follow your system settings
Features:
  • Multiple dark themes
  • System preference detection
  • Easy toggle access
